About Us

Kadri Nrityavidyanilaya Charitable Trust (R.) is a prestigious Bharatanatyam and Classical Music Academy in Mangalore. Under the guidance of **Vidwaan U K Praveen** and **Usha Praveen**, along with our assistant gurus **Vidwaan U P Sharan** & **Vidhushi Nishwitha Sharan**, we are committed to nurturing young talent in Bharatanatyam and Classical Music.

Our Classes

Bharatanatyam Dance Classes 💃

Learn the art of Bharatanatyam from experienced Gurus. Focus on Mudras, Bhavas, and Rhythmic Footwork.

Carnatic Music Classes 🎶

Get trained in Carnatic vocal music under the guidance of highly skilled music teachers.

Kids Classical Dance Classes 👧

Special batches for kids (5-12 years) to help them master Bharatanatyam in a fun and creative way.
