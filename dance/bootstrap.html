About Us

Kadri Nrityavidyanilaya Charitable Trust (R.) is a prestigious Bharatanatyam and Classical Music Academy in Mangalore. Under the guidance of **Vidwaan U K Praveen** and **Usha Praveen**, along with our assistant gurus **Vidwaan U P Sharan** & **Vidhushi Nishwitha Sharan**, we are committed to nurturing young talent in Bharatanatyam and Classical Music.
